01 — The Case for Chicago

Why Chicago?

Chicago is a city unlike any other — third largest in the US, home to a diverse economy where no single industry takes more than 13% share. Build anything here. Hire top talent at cost-efficient rates. And do it surrounded by one of the most supportive founder communities in the country.

Fast Facts

#1 city for nuclear energy

#1 city for women founders

#1 for corporate HQ relocation

#2 largest manufacturing hub nationally

#2 largest number of Fortune 500s in the US

#2 top destination for recent graduates nationally

#3 US metro by GDP — #7 in the world

#3 concentration of data centers in the US

#3 largest AI talent pipeline for core AI majors

#3 for universities with quantum programs

#3 largest concentration of CS degrees nationally

Building America's first-ever quantum campus

Home to the world's most powerful AI supercomputer

Home to Tempus, world's largest AI precision medicine company

Home to the world's largest futures & derivatives exchange

Meeting point for every rail in America — 25% of US freight passes through
